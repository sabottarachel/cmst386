On average our carts will stay frozen 5-6 hours WITHOUT power. A lot of factors play a part such as temperature, placement, etc.. We tell people to keep them plugged in until shortly before your event starts then you can move them where ever you desire and not worry about power.

Around 600 or so, depending on whether they are vegan or dairy. Don't over do it and offer a lot of flavor options, this tends to overwhelm your guests and it will make it difficult for you to access all the flavors within the cart. Six flavors is usually more than enough to make everybody happy.

The carts are very heavy, about 450lbs empty, thus they need to be trailered to you. However, they are on casters making them easy to manipulate and move around. They cannot go up stairs but they easily fit in almost all door jams and elevators. We rarely have a problem getting them to where the need to go.

Here are some other questions we like answered, before we agree to hit the road and attend your event.

  • How many people are expected to attend?
  • Are there other dessert vendors?
  • Will alcohol and/or food be served?
  • What is the demographic makeup of attendees?
  • What time of year is your event?
  • How far away is your event?
  • What fees are associated with your event?
  • Do you require a percentage of sales to be paid or donated?
